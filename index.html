5 Steps for Writing a Polished Resume...

Assembly time: 1 hour
Awesome rating: 100% awesome

Are you struggling to find a job? Whether you're a new graduate or a seasoned professional, a polished resume is essential to a successful job hunt. Follow these 5 simple steps to get started!

  • Things You'll Need

  • Laptop/Desktop
  • Online Document (to write and format)
  • Printer
  • Paper

  1. Start by defining the role you want.

    Tailor your resume to each job, highlighting the skills, education, and experience that make you a strong candidate..

  2. List your relevant jobs with the job title, dates, and place of employment clearly displayed.

    Your resume is a visual document that hiring managers will typically only glance at for a few seconds. Clean and well-structured content can help your resume stand out from the rest.

    An easy-to-read resume can be the difference between getting hired or rejected.

  3. Emphasize hard skills that make you a more valuable employee.

    Brainstorm a list of 4 to 5 categories of skills that you have experience or education in. Focus on hard skills that can be easily evaluated, such as computer or technical skills or languages.

  4. Include relevant education or certifications.

    Typically, your highest degree is the only one you need to include on your resume. However, you should include lower degrees if they're relevant to the job you're applying for. Additionally, list any relevant licenses or certifications you have.

  5. Polish and print your resume.

    Active, punchy text is essential in a resume, since the document will likely only be glanced at for a few seconds. Proofread carefully before submitting, and make sure to print your resume on a good printer with quality paper. Take enough copies so that each interviewer can have one.
